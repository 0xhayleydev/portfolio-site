Tonnow Yn-Mysk (2020)

Falmouth University: Year 1
(GAM110)

This project was developed as part of GAM110's Creative Card Jam during Study Block 1 at Falmouth University, which lasted for 4 weeks.
The theme for this jam was Cornish Mythology and our project Modifier was Escape.
With that in mind, we decided to make a game surrounding the Cornish mythological creature called the Morgawr.
The game sets you as a humble Cornish fisherman.
You must catch as many fish as you can and escape from the clutches of the Morgawr, all before nightfall, where you will be able to upgrade your small fishing vessel ready for the next day.

Credits: Adam Arnett, Alistar Hambly, Ben Hooper, Ben Robinson, George Chandler, Joe Dawes, Michael Sollars, Sho Callanan and Tega Amrore

Nightly Profit Mod
(2018)

Personal: Mod
(Startup Company)


Are you tired of using a calculator to figure out how much money your company is making/losing from its services? Want a way to easily find it out?
Now you can with the Nightly Profit Mod.
This mod creates a new page, showcasing how much money you are making Nightly.
(The mod only calculates how much you make from income on services, how much you spend on servers, staff wages and corporate benefits. It doesn't take into account things like buying furniture or paying one off staff fees when they start working for you).
The mod displays the profit in a new format; GRAPHS, making the old TABLE view obsolete (and thus this has been removed), but I'm sure you won't miss that mess of a display method.
You can also choose how many days of data you want to show, with the options being: 3 Days/5 Days/1 Week/2 Weeks/1 Month /2 Months/1 Year.
